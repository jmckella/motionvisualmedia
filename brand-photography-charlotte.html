Executive brand photography starts at $1,250. The final quote depends on the number of looks, locations, people, and intended usage.

Can you direct people who are uncomfortable on camera?

Yes. Clear direction is part of the work. The goal is to create photographs that feel like you, not stiff poses you have to perform.

Yes. Combined photo and video campaigns can be scoped when the same story or production day needs to serve multiple channels.
