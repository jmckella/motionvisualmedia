Common questions
What is the starting price?

Brand films start at $2,500. Final scope depends on locations, production days, talent, travel, and the intended asset set.

How can a brand film help my business?

A brand film can make an offer easier to understand, introduce the people behind the work, give customers a reason to trust you, or support an important launch or campaign. We start with the communication goal before deciding what the film should look like.

Do you help with the concept?

Yes. We help clarify the message, audience, story, interview direction, visual references, production plan, and delivery needs before the shoot.

Do you work outside Charlotte?

Yes. Motion Visual Media is based in Charlotte and can travel for the right project.
