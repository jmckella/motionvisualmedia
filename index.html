Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Jeremy.

I'm a Charlotte-based filmmaker, and I started Motion Visual Media because I believe the best stories deserve to be told with care — slowly, intentionally, beautifully.

It grew out of McKellar's View — same initials, same obsession with story, sharpened into something I'm prouder of. Whether it's a founder who built something from nothing or a brand finding its voice, my job is the same: find the real story, and make it impossible to look away from.

I work with a small number of people each season. If that's you, I'd love to hear what you're building.
