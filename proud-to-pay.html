Why Proud to Pay

Support the work at a level that feels right.

The name is inspired by Nipsey Hussle's Proud2Pay idea: let people choose how they support work they value.

Every session starts at $100. Choosing a higher amount helps Motion Visual Media cover production costs and make future community portrait days possible. Everyone receives the same session, regardless of the amount they choose.

Being featured is always your choice. You can receive the same portrait experience without giving Motion Visual Media permission to publish your photo or video. Your answer will not affect whether you are considered for a session.
